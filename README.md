### 1. Dynamic Hero Section
- **Visual Impact:** High-resolution hero image background.
- **Trust Elements:** "Awarded the best perfume" badge and customer stats (10x power, 20K+ happy customers).
- **Navigation:** Seamless mobile menu with hamburger toggle and smooth scrolling.

### 2. Interactive Product Gallery
- **Multi-view:** Users can navigate through multiple product angles.
- **Navigation:** Support for Next/Prev buttons, click-to-view thumbnails, and visual dots indicator.
- **Active State:** Smooth transitions between active and inactive images.

### 3. Advanced Purchase System
- **Subscription Models:** Choose between **Single Subscription ($99.99)** or **Double Subscription ($169.99)**.
- **Fragrance Selection:** Interactive cards for "Original", "Lily", and "Rose" with custom radio selection.
- **Dynamic Cart:** The "Add to Cart" button automatically updates its price, URL parameters, and label based on the user's selection.
- **Inclusions Display:** Clear breakdown of what's included (Monthly bottles + Free samplers).

### 4. Interactive Collection Accordion
- **Clean UI:** Collapsible sections for different scent collections.
- **Auto-Close:** Only one section stays open at a time to reduce visual clutter.

### 5. Animated Performance Metrics
- **Scroll-Triggered:** Statistics (Customer Satisfaction, Repurchase Rate, etc.) animate when the user scrolls them into view.
- **Smooth Counting:** Utilizes Intersection Observer for performance-optimized animations.

### 6. Transparency & Comparison
- **Competitive Edge:** A detailed comparison table highlighting why GTG outperforms competitors like Arose, Bella, and Daisies.
- **Value Highlights:** Focus on Concentration, Longevity, and Blend Harmony.
